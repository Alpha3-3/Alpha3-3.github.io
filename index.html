About

I am an undergraduate researcher at the High-Performance Data-Intelligence Computing (HPDIC) Lab, University of Washington. My interests include applied mathematics, data mining, high-dimensional vector search, and machine-learning systems. I work on fast Hausdorff distance approximation and dimension-reduction techniques for efficient nearest-neighbor search.
