About

I’m an undergraduate researcher in the High-Performance Data-Intelligence Computing (HPDIC) Lab at the University of Washington. My interests span applied mathematics, machine learning systems, vector databases, LLMs/NLP, and efficient algorithms for high-dimensional data. Recent projects include ProHD (fast Hausdorff distance approximation supported by PNNL/DOE resources) and MPAD (an unsupervised dimensionality reduction method that preserves nearest-neighbor structure for ANN systems).
